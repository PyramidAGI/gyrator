A user starts with something like:

“Reduce energy use in my home”
“Prepare a proposal for a client”
“Analyze this HR candidate profile”
“Design a small world model explorer”
“Write chapter 2 of my book”

The LLM generates suggestions, questions, plans, summaries, risks, next actions, or drafts. These outputs are not just shown once; they are stored in a structured log. Later, selected log entries are inserted back into the LLM context to improve the next answer.
